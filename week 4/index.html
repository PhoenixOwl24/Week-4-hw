My Favorite Artist


BAD BUNNY

Bad Bunny, Benito Anontio Martínex Ocasio , is a Puerto Rican regggaeton and Latin trap artist who has left an incredible mark on the music industry.
With his infectious energy, electic style, and socially conscious lyrics, Bad Bunny has become a global phenomenon.
From his breakthrough collaborations with artists like J Balvin and Ozuna to his chart-topping solo hits such as "Safaera" and "Dákiti," he has consistenly pushed boundaries and redefined the sound of latin music.
